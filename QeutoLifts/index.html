About Qeuto Lifts

At QEUTO LIFTS, we are committed to redefining vertical transportation with safe, reliable, and high-quality elevator solutions. Since 2012, we have been delivering our own brand of elevators across South India, providing complete turnkey services that include design, component sourcing, assembly, installation, testing, commissioning, and licensing support.

Our strength lies in our technical expertise, strict adherence to Indian Standards (IS), and a strong focus on customer satisfaction. With a growing portfolio of successful installations and a 24/7 service network, we ensure smooth performance and long-term reliability for every project.

Driven by a vision to become a trusted name in the elevator industry, QEUTO LIFTS combines quality, affordability, and service excellence to meet the evolving needs of residential, commercial, and industrial spaces.

Our vision

To become a trusted leader in the elevator industry by delivering safe, reliable, and innovative vertical transportation solutions, while building a strong brand recognized for quality, customer satisfaction, and service excellence.

Our History & Experience

QEUTO LIFTS was established in 2012 with the vision of delivering safe, reliable, and high-quality elevator solutions across South India. Backed by a management and technical team with more than 33 years of industry experience, we have built our foundation on strong technical expertise and customer-centric values.

From the beginning, we specialized in the design, procurement, and assembly of elevators in compliance with Indian Standards (IS). Over the years, our capabilities expanded to provide turnkey solutions, including supply, installation, testing, commissioning, inspection facilitation, and licensing support.

Our portfolio includes a wide range of successful installations such as large-capacity elevators, non-standard lifts tailored to unique building requirements, and modern home lifts designed for residential spaces. This diverse experience allows us to deliver customized solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects alike.

With numerous installations completed and a robust 24/7 service network, QEUTO LIFTS has earned a reputation for technical precision, reliable performance, and customer satisfaction. By combining decades of expertise with innovation, we continue to grow as one of the most trusted names in vertical transportation, with a vision to expand our presence and serve a broader market in the years to come.
